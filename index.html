Instructions

Select how many columns and rows you want to play with followed by the amount of mines you want. The amount of mines must be at most half the amount of squares or it will automatically set them to half the amount of squares. Once you select the amount of mines you want, the game will begin. Each square can contain a number or a mine. The number tells you how many neighboring (diagonally and adjacent) squares contain a mine. if you click on a mine it is gameover and the game will restart with one point being added to loss. You can options(alt) click on a square to add a flag on where you think there is a mine. To win you have to on all the squares that are not mines.
